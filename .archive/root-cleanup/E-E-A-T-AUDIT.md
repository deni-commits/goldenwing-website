## Executive Summary

GoldenWing Creative Studios demonstrates **strong** E-E-A-T signals across most key dimensions. The website successfully establishes credibility through verified client testimonials, detailed company information, professional team profiles, and comprehensive legal documentation. Key strengths include multilingual support (DE/EN/RU), structured data implementation, and transparent business operations. Minor gaps exist in author credentials on blog posts and external authority certifications.
